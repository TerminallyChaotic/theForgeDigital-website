## Accessibility Notes

- **Contrast ratio (text):** All color combinations meet WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 minimum)
- **Reduced motion:** Users with `prefers-reduced-motion` will see simplified color transitions
- **Color independence:** Never rely solely on color to convey information

---

## Brand Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use the cream background as the primary canvas
- Use pine green for primary calls-to-action and brand elements
- Use brass as an accent for visual interest and hierarchy
- Use walnut for text, borders, and structural elements

**Don't:**
- Replace pine green with cooler greens (blue-greens read as tech, not heritage)
- Use gold instead of brass (reads as luxury/flashy, not craft)
- Lighten the cream further (loses warmth and heritage feel)
- Add neon or bright accents (contradicts the understated, trustworthy positioning)
